LEAVE, SCHEDULE, AND MANAGER CONSOLE - DETAILED PLAN

1) SCOPE SUMMARY

Goal: Build a leave management system, employee schedule views, and manager tools for approvals, hierarchy, and shift management. Integrate attendance plant scoring impact per leave type.

Key rules from requirements:
- Employees must select a leave category and can add a note.
- No auto-approval. Managers must approve/reject within 48 hours.
- HR/Admin can override schedules and decisions. Directors/HR/Super Admin can see all employees.
- Notifications: email + in-app + SMS for relevant parties.
- Company holidays are set by HR/Admin (Islamic lunar calendar).
- Leave balances are set per employee by HR/Admin.
- Shifts are flexible (can vary by day of week).
- Leave resets: Jan 1 every year (current policy).
- Partial-day leave is supported.
- Leaves do not accrue or roll over. Unused expired leave converts to points: +10 per unused day.
- Plant score impact: Annual leave and SSP no impact. Sick leave: -1 point per day. Unpaid leave: -2 points per day.
- Annual leave must be requested at least 3 months before the start date.
- Unpaid leave must be requested at least 10 working days before the start date.
- Emergency leave (half day or full day) counts as sick leave.
- Sick leave must be requested at least 4 hours before shift start; employee must call, then manager/HR/admin approves.

3) BUSINESS RULES

3.1 Leave Request Rules
- Employee must choose a leave category.
- Employee can add a note.
- Partial days allowed: specify start/end time or hours.
- Annual leave minimum notice: 3 months before start date.
- Unpaid leave minimum notice: 10 working days before start date.
- Emergency leave (half day or full day) is recorded as sick leave.
- Sick leave must be requested at least 4 hours before shift start; call required, then manager/HR/admin approval.
- No auto approval. Manager must act within 48 hours.
- HR/Admin can override.

3.2 Approval Flow
- Submit -> Manager review within 48 hours.
- If HR required (leave_type.requires_hr), it must also be approved by HR.
- HR/Admin can override at any time.

3.3 Plant Score Impact
- Annual leave, SSP, bereavement, maternity, paternity: 0 points per day.
- Sick leave: -1 point per day.
- Unpaid leave: -2 points per day.
- Partial day impact is proportional by hours (e.g., 0.5 day = -0.5 or -1 points).

3.4 Leave Reset and Expiry
- Reset annually on Jan 1 (configurable by HR/Admin).
- Unused days expire and convert to points: +10 per unused day.
- No roll-over.

3.5 Operational Enhancements
- Leave blackout rules (no annual leave during peak periods), with HR override flag.
- Leave quotas by role/department to reduce manual balance setup.
- Leave clash warnings (max employees out per team/day).
- Coverage score to show staffing risk before approving leave.
- Pending aging banner at 24h/36h to avoid SLA breaches.
- Full audit trail for overrides and schedule changes.
- Leave expiry warnings: auto-notify employees 30/14/7 days before deadline.
- Calendar heatmap showing team capacity (red/yellow/green staffing % by date).
- Department minimum staffing rules: block approval if coverage would breach minimum.
- Sick leave pattern flagging: alert HR to suspicious patterns.
- Substitute finder: suggest cover when leave is approved.
- Manager delegation: manager can assign approval authority while away.
- Shift swap system: employees propose peer swaps; manager approves once.
- Leave analytics dashboard: trends, costs, department breakdowns.
- Payroll integration: track SSP vs company-paid for accounting.

6) NOTIFICATIONS

Channels: in-app, email, SMS for all relevant parties.

Events:
- New request submitted -> manager + employee
- Manager decision -> employee + HR (if required)
- HR decision -> employee + manager
- Override -> employee + manager + HR
- 24-hour reminder if pending
- 48-hour SLA breach alert to HR

7) ATTENDANCE PLANT INTEGRATION

- Daily cron job calculates plant points using leave records.
- Approved leave for day excludes normal attendance scoring.
- Sick leave applies -1 per day, unpaid leave -2 per day, partial days pro-rated.
- Annual leave and SSP have zero impact.
- Expired unused leave yields +10 points per day at reset.
