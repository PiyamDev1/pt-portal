HR DATA FOUNDATION & EMPLOYEE MANAGEMENT - DETAILED PLAN

1) SCOPE SUMMARY

Goal: Build comprehensive HR data infrastructure supporting leave management, payroll, compliance, performance, and employee lifecycle management.

Phases span employee onboarding through performance reviews, disciplinary tracking, training compliance, and offboarding workflows.

3) BUSINESS RULES

3.1 Employment Basics
- Contract type determines leave entitlements and notice periods.
- Employment status controls system access and leave approvals.
- Probation period: limited leave during first 3-6 months (configurable).
- Notice period is validated when termination is processed.

3.2 Salary & Payroll
- Salary is used to calculate leave costs and SSP eligibility.
- SSP daily rate is set by UK rules annually, validated on sick leave approval.
- Bank details encrypted for payroll protection.
- Salary currency allows multi-currency support (future).

3.3 Compliance & Right-to-Work
- Right-to-work must be verified before employment starts.
- Documents (offer, contract, handbook) must be acknowledged.
- Background check status required before user access.
- Training compliance tracked; non-compliance flagged to HR.

3.4 Disciplinary Tracking
- Attendance plant warnings link to disciplinary records.
- 3 warnings trigger escalation (already in plant plan).
- Records are permanent audit trail.

3.5 Performance Management
- Reviews tied to salary review recommendations.
- Goals and development areas tracked annually.
- Optional: link to bonuses/raises in Phase 3+.

3.6 Onboarding/Offboarding
- Onboarding tasks create checklist for HR/manager pre-hire.
- Offboarding tasks ensure clean exit: equipment, references, final pay.
- Exit reason tracked for analytics.
